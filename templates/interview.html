🌟 Interview Tips

→ Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for behavioural questions.
→ It's okay to think before answering. Interviewers value structured thinking.
→ Ask clarifying questions — it shows analytical thinking and communication skills.
→ Be honest about knowledge gaps; explain how you would find the answer.
